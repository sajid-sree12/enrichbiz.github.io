How efficient is your hospital or pharmaceutical’s supply chain ?

AI Led Supply Chain Management (SCM): Healthcare costs are increasing in every market and putting pressure on hospitals. CXO’s are now turning their attention to their supply chain which traditionally was not a focus area
Our AI based supply chain management solution helps reduce costs, improve patient experience, provide data-driven insights and improves overall operational efficiencies. This holistic solution presents an opportunity to improve a hospital’s margin by 5-7%


A glimpse of today’s healthcare supply chain….

  1. Hospital supplies account for about 1/3rd of overall operating expenses

  2. Supplies predicted to exceed labor costs in almost all markets

  3. Provider preference (Physician preference) items typically account for 56% of supply

  4. Burden on healthcare personnel due to lack of right supply during Perioperative and procedural throughput

  5. Expired or recalled supplies ~10% wastage

Limitations of current SCM systems


  • Hospitals largely are using traditional ERP solutions which use time series to predict future. Using past patterns does not factor in seasonal and other factors leading to “wastage and avoidable high costs”

  • Complexities in healthcare supply chain result in overstock, understock, idle stock and wastage.

  • There are many good inventory systems developed or implemented by leading hospitals and pharmaceutical chains however, ’human resources’ are required to analyze, decide and act.
